What is Food & Nutrition

Food and Nutrition is an elective program, that trains and educate students to sharpen their knowledge and skills on cooking. Teachers teaches students the proper and correct way to handle utensils and ingredients in the kitchen.

The Teacher

Mr. Febrian is a trained proffesional experienced chef that teaches students the correct and proper way of cooking. He has been teaching Food & Nutrition over 5 years and has been appreciated for his hard work and talent by many students. 

What we learn?

Food and Nutrition provides students with the most aboslute proffesional way in cooking. Students are taught how to cook spesific foods and are taught on the importance of food for humans. It's bad and good side.
